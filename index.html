ABOUT ME

My name is Atikah, frontend web developer from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. I have a passion for crafting engaging and user-friendly websites that prioritize both functionality and design. My journey into programming began with a fascination for technology, which led me to pursue the CS50X: Introduction to Computer Science course from Harvard. This foundational experience ignited my interest in web development and inspired me to dive deeper into the world of frontend engineering. To hone my skills in creating intuitive user experiences, I embarked on the Google UX Design course, where I learned the principles and methodologies behind effective interface design. This knowledge has been invaluable in shaping my approach to building websites and applications that seamlessly blend form and function.
